# AGENTS.md - Business Personas for Light Ocean Studios

This document defines the key "agents" or personas responsible for managing the various aspects of the Light Ocean Studios business. Each agent has a specific role and set of responsibilities.

For the overall business strategy, to-dos, and next steps, please refer to the [BUSINESS_BRAIN.md](./BUSINESS_BRAIN.md) file.

## The AGENTS

To effectively manage the business, we can define several key "agents" or personas, each with specific responsibilities.

### a. **The Visionary (CEO & Creative Director)**
*   **Role:** Guides the overall brand vision, product development, and creative direction.
*   **Responsibilities:**
    *   Defines the brand's long-term strategy.
    *   Develops new scent profiles and product ideas.
    *   Ensures all marketing and product efforts align with the brand's core values.
    *   Manages the business's finances and legal aspects.

### b. **The Marketer (CMO & Content Strategist)**
*   **Role:** Builds brand awareness, drives traffic to the website, and converts visitors into customers.
*   **Responsibilities:**
    *   Manages social media presence (Instagram, Pinterest are key for this aesthetic).
    *   Develops and executes email marketing campaigns.
    *   Creates content (blog posts, social media updates, newsletters) that aligns with the brand's meditative tone.
    *   Manages SEO and online advertising.

### c. **The Operator (COO & Production Manager)**
*   **Role:** Manages the day-to-day operations of the business, from production to shipping.
*   **Responsibilities:**
    *   Sources raw materials (wax, wicks, fragrances, jars).
    *   Manages inventory of finished products and raw materials.
    *   Oversees the candle pouring and packaging process.
    *   Manages order fulfillment and shipping.
    *   Handles customer service inquiries.

### d. **The Technologist (CTO & Web Developer)**
*   **Role:** Manages the technical infrastructure of the business, including the website and e-commerce platform.
*   **Responsibilities:**
    *   Implements the e-commerce functionality on the website.
    *   Integrates the email marketing service.
    *   Ensures the website is secure, fast, and reliable.
    *   Monitors website analytics and reports on performance.
